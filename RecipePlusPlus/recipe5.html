Zukini Bites





Description:

These cheesy zucchini patties are an excellent way to use up that abundance of zucchini from the garden.
Serve with a bit of tomato sauce or sour cream dabbed on top.

Serves : 4

Ingredients:

    - 2 cups grated zucchini
    - 2 eggs, beaten
    - 1/4 cup chopped onion
    - 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    - 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    - 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    - salt to taste
    - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Direction:

  *  In a medium bowl, combine the zucchini, eggs, onion, flour, Parmesan cheese,
     mozzarella cheese, and salt. Stir well enough to distribute ingredients evenly.

  *  Heat a small amount of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.

  *  Drop zucchini mixture by heaping tablespoonfuls, and cook for a few minutes
     on each side until golden.

  *  Serve!
